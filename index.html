Public Value
Technology Accelerator

The Public Value Technology Accelerator brings together students from the Next Generation Service Corps
and Fulton Schools of Engineering at Arizona State University to jointly develop mobile applications that generate
social value. Students come to the PVTA upon completion of a course where they design and prototype a mobile
application that addresses a community-based problem in Phoenix.
